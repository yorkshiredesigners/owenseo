What is AI SEO?

AI SEO is about making sure your business can still be found as search changes. People are no longer only typing short keywords into Google. They are asking longer questions, comparing options inside AI tools and expecting direct answers before they ever land on a website.

That does not mean normal SEO is dead. Far from it. Strong technical SEO, useful content, reviews, links, brand mentions and clear service pages still matter. The difference is that your website and wider brand now need to make sense to search engines and AI answer engines.

My approach is simple: keep the SEO foundations strong, then adapt the content, structure and brand signals around the way people are searching now.

Search is Changing Fast

Pew Research Center found that AI summaries appeared in 18% of Google searches in its March 2025 study, and that longer searches of 10 words or more triggered an AI summary 53% of the time. That matters because those longer searches are often the ones where people are asking proper questions.

Bain and Company also reported that ChatGPT shopping related prompts grew by 25% in the first half of 2025. In plain English, people are not just using AI for fun. They are using it to research products, compare options and make decisions.

This is why AI SEO is the future. It is not about chasing a buzzword. It is about making sure your business is easy to understand, easy to trust and easy to recommend wherever your customers are searching.

My Approach to AI SEO

I do not use AI as a shortcut to push out weak content. That is not a strategy. I use it where it helps with analysis, research, patterns and speed, but the thinking still needs to come from someone who understands SEO and your business.

The first step is to review how your business currently performs in normal search and how well your content answers the questions people are likely to ask in AI tools. From there I look at your service pages, FAQs, supporting content, reviews, brand mentions, schema, technical setup and competitor position.

The end result is a clearer plan for both search engines and AI search. Better pages, better answers, stronger trust signals and a site that is easier to understand.

AI SEO FAQs

Here are some of the most common questions I get about AI SEO.

What is AI SEO?

AI SEO is the process of improving your website and wider brand presence so you can perform better in traditional search engines and be more likely to appear in AI search experiences such as Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot and Perplexity.

Is AI SEO different from normal SEO?

It is different, but it does not replace normal SEO. Strong technical SEO, useful content, brand mentions, reviews, links and clear answers all still matter. AI SEO builds on those foundations and looks at how people search inside AI tools too.

Will AI replace SEO?

No. AI is changing how people search, but businesses still need clear websites, useful content, technical SEO and a strong brand footprint. The job is to adapt SEO for both search engines and AI search tools.

What does an AI SEO service include?

An AI SEO service can include an AI search audit, traditional SEO audit, content reviews, FAQ improvements, structured data, brand mention checks, review checks, competitor research and tracking how your brand appears across search engines and AI tools.

How long does AI SEO take?

It depends on your current website, brand strength, content, competitors and how quickly changes can be implemented. Some improvements can be seen quickly, but building trust across search engines and AI tools usually takes consistent work over several months.
