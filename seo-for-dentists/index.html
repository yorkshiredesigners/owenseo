SEO For Dentists FAQs

Common questions about SEO for dental practices, local rankings, bookings and how I work.

What is SEO for dentists?

SEO for dentists is the process of improving a dental practice website so it can be found by people searching for treatments, urgent appointments and dental services in the areas the practice serves.

Can SEO help my dental practice get more bookings?

Yes. The aim is to attract people who are already searching for dental care, then make it easier for them to call, enquire or book with your practice.

Which dental services can SEO target?

SEO can target emergency dentistry, dental implants, Invisalign, teeth whitening, general dentistry, hygiene appointments, cosmetic dentistry and private dentistry, depending on what your practice offers.

How long does dental SEO take to work?

It depends on competition, your current website and how quickly changes are made. I would usually hope to see early movement within the first few months, but stronger results normally need consistent work.

Can you help with local SEO for dentists?

Yes. Local SEO is usually a key part of SEO for dentists because most patients search by location. This can include treatment pages, local landing pages, Google Business Profile recommendations and tracking.

How much do you charge for dental SEO?

I charge £40 per hour, so the cost depends on how much work is needed, how competitive your area is and how quickly you want to move.
